Welcome to Gold Coast Backpackers!

Gold Coast Backpackers is the ultimate place for all backpackers to relax an enjoy the Surf, sand, nature, adventure, shopping, nightlife & theme parks of the amazing Gold Coast and South East Queensland.

Gold Coast Backpackers is a stones throw from the white sandy beaches of Surfers Paradise where you can learn to surf, do the SkyPoint climb on Australia’s highest external building; the iconic Q1, shop till you drop at the magnificent Cavill avenue or dance the night away in the stylish pubs, clubs and lounge bars of Surfers Paradise.

Gold Coast Backpackers is a purpose built backpackers offering a variety of dorms at a budget price. We offer you the experience of home away from home

We have fully licensed bar so you can relax and enjoy the cable overage all around the common areas or play a friendly game of pool with your fellow travellers or cook up a storm on the BBQ out on the terrace.

We also offer free Wi-Fi, secure parking and laundry facilities. The rooms we have on offer are 2, 3 or 4 bed Dorms which have their own private bathroom, wall fan, steel lockers to lock up your valuables and own private balcony.

All Guests Please Note!

Guest must be 18 year or older to stay with us and must present their passports or a valid id during check-in when staying at the backpackers.

Please note we do not accept any schoolies booking and any such bookings made will not be honoured and may not be refunded.
